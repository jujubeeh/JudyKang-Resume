Adept in assessing the mental and behavioral health of children, keeping record of progress, and suggesting powerful solutions to issues. Committed to reinforcing positive feelings and behaviors in patients, thus helping children to lead healthier and happier lives.

Committed to helping children get in touch with their positive feelings and behaviors and therefore leading them to lead happier, healthier and more meaningful lives. I specialize in children suffering from depression, substance abuse, ADHD, ADD, PTSD, anxiety and schizophrenia.
